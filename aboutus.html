About Afrotrade

Afrotrade Corporation Limited is a diversified trading and distribution company strategically positioned at Plot 13969, Chandwe Musonda Road, Light Industrial Area, Lusaka, Zambia.

The company operates across two vital sectors – energy and agriculture – supplying petroleum products, industrial oils, chemicals, and agricultural commodities to both domestic and regional markets.

Our Vision

To become a leading regional supplier of energy and agricultural solutions, recognized for excellence, innovation, and sustainable impact in Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable, high-quality energy and agricultural products that fuel growth, enhance trade, and create value for communities, partners, and stakeholders.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Delivering products and services that exceed industry standards.

Integrity

Upholding trust, transparency, and accountability in all business dealings.

Sustainability

Promoting environmentally responsible practices and long-term growth.

Innovation

Continuously adapting to meet the evolving needs of our markets.

Why Choose Afrotrade?

  • Licensed and compliant with all regulatory bodies
  • Strategic location in Lusaka for efficient distribution
  • Diversified portfolio bridging both energy and agriculture
  • Strong commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability
  • A trusted partner across Zambia and beyond
